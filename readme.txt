Show customers how much they can save.

== Description ==

The Calculate Customer's Savings plugin shows customers how much they can save in an Order.

The Calculate Customer's Savings plugin allows you to show your customers how much they can save on their orders. The plugin works by calculating the savings based on the products that are currently on sale, and displays the savings on both the cart and checkout pages.

This feature not only helps to increase customer satisfaction by providing them with real-time savings information, but it can also boost sales by encouraging customers to add more items to their order. With the Calculate Customer's Savings plugin, you can provide your customers with a better shopping experience while also increasing your bottom line.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How is the savings amount calculated? =

The plugin calculates the savings amount based on the price of products that are currently on sale. It does not include other types of discounts such as coupons or bulk purchase discounts. Although, including other types of discounts is a feature that could be implemented in the future updates.
